Hi! I'm Luke, a UX Researcher completing my PhD
in Human-Computer Interaction at LMU Munich in Germany.

My research focuses on technologies for wellbeing and the future of work. Previously, I've co-founded a company and worked at an early-stage neurotech startup. I have a masters in Biomedical Engineering from UBC in Vancouver and a bachelors in Mechanical Engineering from Dalhousie University.

About Me

I'm an avid adventurer. In summers I love long distance hiking, camping, and bouldering. When the weather gets frosty I freeride and backcountry ski and play hockey. I also travel, practice yoga, and (try to) play guitar.
I was born and raised in the small town of Antigonish on the east coast of Canada. Since then, I've lived around the world in Halifax, Vancouver, Shenzhen, and Munich.
